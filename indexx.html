FAQ

How long does review take?⌄

Most reviews happen within a couple of days. If you include links and context, it is faster.

Do I need prior experience?⌄

Not necessarily. Show willingness to learn and examples of initiative, even small ones.

Can I reapply?⌄

Yes. If it was not a fit, feel free to reapply after you have grown your skills or portfolio.

Where will you contact me?⌄

We typically reach out via Discord using the tag you provide in the application.
